  [ ] Terminal you use     (goal: iTerm2 — quit Terminal.app, pin iTerm2)
  [ ] Night Shift schedule (System Settings > Displays > Night Shift)
  [ ] Menu-bar item order  (Sonoma+ stores much of this outside defaults)
